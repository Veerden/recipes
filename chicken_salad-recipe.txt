Salt and pepper 6 to 8-chicken breasts in a dish capable of being in the oven.  Bake the chicken breasts at 400-degrees for 1-hour, or until cooked through.  Remove chicken breasts and shred into a large mixing bowl.  Add to taste mayonnaise, sweet relish, and any other seasonings you would like.  Once shredded chicken and other ingredients are mixed well.  Toast two pieces of bread, spread a spoon full of chicken salad onto the toasted bread, and ENJOY!!
